FAQ

What is NFT Crypto?

Non-changeable tokens (NFTs) are not like cryptocurrencies, although they are a digital asset. Their value comes from how rare they are, so some go for millions of dollars. NFT is the future of art.

How can I buy NFT?

You can buy NFTs from OpenSea, Rarible and Mintable.

What is NFT worth?

These cryptoassets represent the latest blockchain-based boom: Three years ago, the entire NFT market was worth no more than $42 million. By the end of 2020, it had grown 705% to $338 million in value, according to the latest estimate from Nonfungible.com, which monitors the NFT marketplace.
